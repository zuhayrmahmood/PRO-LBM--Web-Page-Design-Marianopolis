What I like about Basketball

Basketball is one of my favourite sports. I really like the team aspect of basketball.
Everyone is constantly involved and it is the perfect mix of brains and athleticism.
There are also many different oppurtunities for spectacular 200IQ plays to be made.

How I feel playing Basketball

It has been a long time since I've played basketball. But back in my hayday I was the MVP of our team during high school.
Before COVID, I also played at Marianopolis. I am always ina blissful state while playing. It taked my mind off of the trying times of life.

How I started liking Basketball

Originally, I was not a fan of Basketball at all. I started playign when I was 6 but kept on playing. I only started enjoying Basketball once I grew during my teen years.
Basketball is an extremely appealing sport, many people enjoy it and it is one of the top grossing sports in the world.
Hopefully I can play once again with my friends (one of the reasons why I played) soon enough thanks to COVID.
