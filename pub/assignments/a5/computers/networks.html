Networks

Computers use networks all the time to communicate with each other (PC to PC, PC to server etc.). There are multiple types of networks but one of the most popular are LAN, or Local Area Network. This style of networks links multiple PCs within a small area, like a house or an office building. These networks are typically quite secure. Video games use LAN all the time, in fact, there are events called "LAN parties" where people bring their PCs to each other's houses (not so much anymore because of COVID) and play on a LAN, reducing lag. LAN parties are very popular for online games such as VALORANT or Overwatch. (Original Work)
